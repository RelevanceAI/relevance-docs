At Relevance, you can upload variety of data sources to be used by AI as knowledge or surces of 
truth. 
You are not limitted to one or two data formats but many such as PDF, CSV, Audio, Websites, 
as well as a huge range of integrations such as Figma, Zendesk and YouTube are supported.

Click on `+ New knowledge set`button and you will be prompted with the upload knowledge flow.
Any data that is uploaded as knowledge in any of your chains will also be stored and shown under this page.
