## What is the Desktop App?

The Desktop App runs natively on your Mac and automatically detects when you're in a meeting. It captures audio directly from your device, transcribes conversations in real-time, and syncs everything to your Relevance AI account where you can interact with the content using AI chat.

## Key benefits

### Privacy-first recording
Your meeting audio is recorded locally on your device. You control what gets recorded and when, without relying on cloud bots that join as visible participants.

### Works with any meeting platform
The Desktop App captures system audio, so it works with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and any other video conferencing tool you use.

### Real-time insights
Transcripts appear as conversations happen, and you can start chatting with the AI about meeting content immediately after the call ends.

### Seamless sync
Recordings and transcripts automatically sync to your Relevance AI account, making them accessible across your team and integrating with your existing workflows.

## Desktop App vs Meeting Notetaker Agent

Relevance AI offers two ways to record and transcribe meetings. Here's how they compare:

| Feature | Desktop App | Meeting Notetaker Agent |
|---------|-------------|------------------------|
| **Recording method** | Local recording on your Mac | Cloud bot joins meeting |
| **Visibility** | Invisible to other participants | Visible as meeting participant |
| **Platform support** | Any meeting platform | Zoom, Google Meet, Teams |
| **Setup required** | Install app + grant permissions | Connect calendar + configure triggers |
| **Best for** | Personal recording & privacy | Team-wide automation & distribution |

<Tip>
Use the **Desktop App** when you want private, local recording that works with any platform. Use the **[Meeting Notetaker Agent](/build/agents/use-cases/meeting-notetaker)** when you need automated meeting attendance and team-wide distribution of summaries.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do other meeting participants know I'm recording?">
    The Desktop App records locally on your device and doesn't join meetings as a visible participant. However, you should always inform participants when recording meetings to comply with consent laws in your jurisdiction.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does the Desktop App work with all video conferencing platforms?">
    Yes. Because it captures system audio directly from your Mac, it works with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and any other platform you use.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where are my recordings stored?">
    Recordings are initially stored locally on your Mac, then automatically synced to your Relevance AI account in the cloud. You can access them from any device where you're logged into Relevance AI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use the Desktop App on Windows?">
    The Desktop App is currently only available for macOS. Windows support is planned for a future release.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How much does the Desktop App cost?">
    The Desktop App is included with your Relevance AI subscription. Transcription and AI chat features consume credits based on your usage.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
