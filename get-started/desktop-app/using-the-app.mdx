The Relevance AI Desktop App automatically detects when you're in a meeting and handles recording, transcription, and syncing in the background. This guide covers everything you need to know about using the app day-to-day.

## Automatic meeting detection

The Desktop App monitors your active applications and automatically detects when you join a meeting.

### How detection works

The app recognizes common video conferencing platforms:
- Zoom
- Google Meet (Chrome, Safari, or standalone app)
- Microsoft Teams
- Webex
- Any other meeting platform running in a browser or dedicated app

When you join a meeting, the app will:
1. Display a notification that a meeting has been detected
2. Show a recording indicator in the menu bar
3. Begin capturing audio automatically (if auto-record is enabled)

<Tip>
You can configure automatic recording in the app settings. If disabled, you'll need to manually start recording when a meeting is detected.
</Tip>

## Starting and stopping recordings

### Automatic recording

If automatic recording is enabled:

1. Join a meeting in any supported platform
2. The app detects the meeting and starts recording automatically
3. A recording indicator appears in the menu bar
4. Transcription begins in real-time

### Manual recording

To start recording manually:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the menu bar icon">
    Click the Relevance AI icon in your macOS menu bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start recording">
    Click "Start Recording" in the dropdown menu.
    
    The app will begin capturing audio and transcribing in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Stop recording

To stop a recording:

1. Click the Relevance AI icon in your menu bar
2. Click "Stop Recording"
3. The app will finalize the recording and sync it to your account

<Note>
Recordings automatically stop when you leave a meeting or close the video conferencing app. You can also set a maximum recording duration in the app settings.
</Note>

## Viewing live transcripts

While a meeting is being recorded, you can view the transcript in real-time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the transcript view">
    Click the Relevance AI menu bar icon and select "View Transcript."
    
    A window will open showing the live transcript as it's being generated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow along">
    The transcript updates in real-time as people speak, with speaker labels and timestamps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and navigate">
    Use the search box to find specific words or phrases in the transcript.
    
    Click any timestamp to jump to that point in the recording.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Live transcripts are generated using AI and may contain errors, especially with technical terms or accents. The transcript is refined after the meeting ends for improved accuracy.
</Info>

## Interacting with AI chat

After a meeting ends, you can chat with AI about the meeting content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the recording">
    Open the Relevance AI web app and navigate to your recordings, or click "Open in Chat" from the Desktop App menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Ask questions about the meeting:
    - "What were the main action items?"
    - "Summarize the discussion about the Q4 roadmap"
    - "Who mentioned the budget concerns?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get instant insights">
    The AI analyzes the full transcript and provides contextual answers with references to specific moments in the conversation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can also @ mention the recording in Relevance Chat to include meeting context in broader conversations with other agents.
</Tip>

## How syncing works

The Desktop App automatically syncs recordings and transcripts to your Relevance AI account.

### What gets synced

- Audio recordings
- Full transcripts with speaker labels and timestamps
- Meeting metadata (date, duration, participants if available)
- Any notes or highlights you add

### When syncing happens

- **During recording**: Transcripts sync in real-time as they're generated
- **After recording**: The complete audio file uploads in the background
- **Continuous**: Any changes you make to notes or highlights sync immediately

### Sync status

Check sync status in the Desktop App:

1. Click the menu bar icon
2. Look for the sync indicator at the bottom of the menu
3. Green checkmark = fully synced
4. Spinning icon = syncing in progress
5. Warning icon = sync issue (click for details)

<Warning>
Large recordings may take several minutes to upload, especially on slower internet connections. Keep the Desktop App running until sync completes.
</Warning>

## Managing recordings

### View all recordings

To see your recording history:

1. Click the Relevance AI menu bar icon
2. Select "View Recordings"
3. Browse your recordings by date, duration, or title

### Rename recordings

Recordings are automatically titled with the date and time. To rename:

1. Right-click a recording in the list
2. Select "Rename"
3. Enter a descriptive title
4. Press Enter to save

### Delete recordings

To delete a recording:

1. Right-click the recording in the list
2. Select "Delete"
3. Confirm deletion in the dialog

<Note>
Deleting a recording from the Desktop App also removes it from your Relevance AI account. This action cannot be undone.
</Note>

## App settings

Customize the Desktop App behavior in settings:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open settings">
    Click the menu bar icon and select "Settings" (or press Cmd + ,).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure options">
    Available settings include:
    
    **Recording**
    - Automatic recording on/off
    - Audio quality (standard, high, maximum)
    - Maximum recording duration
    
    **Detection**
    - Which apps to monitor for meetings
    - Notification preferences
    - Auto-start recording delay
    
    **Sync**
    - Sync frequency
    - Upload on cellular data (on/off)
    - Storage location for local recordings
    
    **Privacy**
    - Pause recording during screen sharing
    - Exclude specific apps from detection
    - Local recording retention period
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Settings save automatically as you change them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for better recordings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a good microphone">
    While the Desktop App works with your Mac's built-in microphone, an external microphone or headset will produce clearer audio and more accurate transcripts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Minimize background noise">
    Record in a quiet environment when possible. Background noise can affect transcription accuracy.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Check your audio input">
    Before important meetings, verify the correct microphone is selected in macOS System Settings > Sound > Input.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Test recording first">
    Do a test recording before your first important meeting to ensure everything is working correctly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Keep the app running">
    Don't quit the Desktop App during meetings. It needs to run in the background to detect and record meetings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monitor storage space">
    Recordings are stored locally before syncing. Make sure you have adequate disk space, especially for long meetings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Recording best practices

### Before the meeting

- Verify permissions are granted
- Check that automatic recording is enabled (if desired)
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection for syncing
- Test your microphone input

### During the meeting

- Keep the Desktop App running in the background
- Don't force quit or restart your Mac
- Monitor the recording indicator to confirm recording is active
- Use the pause feature if discussing sensitive information

### After the meeting

- Wait for the recording to finish syncing before closing your laptop
- Review the transcript for accuracy
- Add notes or highlights while the meeting is fresh in your mind
- Share the recording with team members if needed
