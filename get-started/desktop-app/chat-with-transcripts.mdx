The Relevance AI desktop app automatically creates a chat session when you start recording a meeting. This lets you ask questions, get summaries, and interact with your meeting transcript as it's being captured.

## How it works

When you start recording a meeting in the desktop app, a chat session is automatically created and linked to your live transcript. You can open this chat at any time during or after the meeting to interact with the content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start recording">
    Begin recording your meeting in the desktop app. A chat session is created automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the chat">
    Click **Open in chat** in the desktop app to launch the chat session in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interact with your transcript">
    Ask questions, request summaries, or extract action items from your meeting transcript.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Opening your chat

The desktop app provides quick access to your meeting chat:

- **During a recording**: Click **Open in chat** to open the active chat session
- **When idle**: Click **Open last meeting in chat** to return to your most recent meeting's chat

The chat opens in your default browser at [chat.relevanceai.com](https://chat.relevanceai.com), where you have access to the full Relevance Chat experience.

## What you can do in chat

Once your chat is open, you can:

- **Ask questions** about what was discussed in the meeting
- **Get summaries** of key points or specific topics
- **Extract action items** and decisions from the conversation
- **@ mention other agents** to perform additional tasks with the transcript
- **Switch LLMs** to use different models for different types of analysis

The chat has full context of your meeting transcript, so you can reference specific moments, speakers, or topics naturally.

<Tip>
You can use any agent in your project alongside your meeting transcript. Try @ mentioning agents for tasks like drafting follow-up emails, creating task lists, or analyzing sentiment.
</Tip>

## Chat and transcript sync

Your chat session stays linked to the meeting transcript. As the recording continues, the chat has access to the latest transcript content, so you can ask questions about what just happened in real-time.

After the meeting ends, the chat remains available with the complete transcript, so you can return to it later for reference or further analysis.

## Using chat features

Your meeting chat has access to all standard [Relevance Chat](/get-started/chat/introduction) features:

- **LLM selection**: Choose which model to use for different types of queries
- **Agent integration**: @ mention any agent in your project
- **File handling**: Upload additional context or export chat outputs
- **Saved prompts**: Use team-wide prompt templates for common meeting tasks

<Note>
The desktop app records meetings locally on your device. This is different from the Meeting Notetaker Agent, which joins meetings as a cloud-based bot.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I access the chat from multiple devices?">
    Yes. The chat session is available at chat.relevanceai.com, so you can access it from any device with a browser. Your transcript and chat history are synced across devices.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I close the chat during a recording?">
    The chat session remains active and continues to sync with your transcript. You can reopen it at any time by clicking **Open in chat** in the desktop app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I share the chat with my team?">
    Chat sessions follow your project's sharing settings. Team members with access to your project can view and interact with shared chat sessions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long are chat sessions stored?">
    Chat sessions are stored according to your project's data retention settings. You can access past meeting chats through your chat history at chat.relevanceai.com.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
