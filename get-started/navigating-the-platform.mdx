The Relevance AI platform has two main navigation elements: the sidebar on the left and the profile avatar dropdown in the top right. This page explains how each works.

## The sidebar

The sidebar gives you access to all the main areas of the platform: Agents, Tools, Knowledge, Workforces, Marketplace, and more.

### Collapsing and expanding the sidebar

You can minimize the sidebar to free up screen space. Click the **minimize button** (the arrow icon at the bottom of the sidebar) to collapse it. When collapsed, the sidebar shows icons only — hover over any icon to see its label in a tooltip.

To expand the sidebar again, click the same button.

<Tip>
Your sidebar preference is saved automatically. If you collapse the sidebar and reload the page or start a new session, it stays collapsed.
</Tip>

## Profile avatar dropdown

Your profile avatar is in the top right corner of the platform. Click it to open a dropdown with account and workspace controls.

From the profile dropdown you can:

- **Switch projects or regions** — select a different project or region from the selector. This was moved here from the sidebar in a recent update.
- **Access profile settings** — update your name, email, and other account details.
- **Manage your team** — invite members and configure permissions.
- **Sign out** — log out of your account.

<Note>
If you have access to multiple projects, use the project selector in the profile dropdown to switch between them. See [How to switch projects](/admin/project-management/switch-projects) for step-by-step instructions.
</Note>
