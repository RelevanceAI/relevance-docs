---

At this time, the only way to delete a Relevance AI project is to reach out to our support team.

You can learn how to contact our support team at [here](https://relevanceai.com/docs/get-started/support).

<Warning>Project deletion is **permanent**.</Warning>

<Note>Adding the ability to self-serve project deletion is on our roadmap!</Note>
