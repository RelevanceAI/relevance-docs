## Changing the name of a project

<Info>To change the name of a project, you must be an admin of the project.</Info>

To change the name of a project, you should do the following:

1. Click on your user icon in the top left of your workspace.
2. Click on `Manage organization`.
3. Find the project you want to change the name of and expand it.
4. Click the option menu for your project and select `Edit`.
5. Change the name or description of your project.
6. Click `Save`!

## Changing the name of your organization

<Info>To change the name of your organization, you must be an admin of the organization.</Info>

To change the name of your organization, you should do the following:

1. Click on your user icon in the top left of your workspace.
2. Click on `Manage organization`.
3. Click `Plan & Billing`.
4. Click the pencil icon next to your organization name.
5. Change the name of your organization.
6. Click the tick icon to save your changes!
