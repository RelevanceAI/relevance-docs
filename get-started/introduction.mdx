Whether you're looking to automate customer support, scale your sales operations, or streamline internal workflows, Relevance AI provides the tools and infrastructure to build, deploy, and manage your AI workforce.

## How It Works

Building your AI workforce is straightforward:

1. **Define the role** - Describe what you want your agent to do in plain English
2. **Connect your tools** - Give agents access to the systems they need (CRM, email, databases, etc.)
3. **Add knowledge** - Upload documents, connect data sources, or integrate with your knowledge base
4. **Set guardrails** - Configure approval workflows, escalation paths, and human oversight
5. **Deploy and monitor** - Launch your agent and track performance in real-time
