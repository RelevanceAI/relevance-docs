## Signing in

Tap **Enterprise login** to open the sign-in page in a web view. The mobile apps support email and password, SSO, Google, and Apple sign-in.

## Not available in the mobile app

A few features are web-only for now. Use the browser extension or desktop app if you need them.

- **Edge regions** (US Edge, AU Edge, EU Edge) — available in the [Chat browser extension and desktop app](/admin/project-management/switch-projects#selecting-an-edge-region-in-chat) only.
- **Browser extension features** — page-aware actions that rely on the extension itself.
- **Keyboard shortcuts** — the shortcuts listed in the Chat overview don't apply on mobile.
- **Clipboard paste for files and images** — use the camera or attachment picker instead.

## Settings on mobile

Open the settings menu to manage your account and customize Chat:

- **Account** — view the signed-in user and sign out.
- **Workspace** — switch projects, see your organization and region (read-only).
- **Chat** — pick the model used for new conversations.
- **Preferences** — set the thinking level (Off, Standard, Extended) and choose a light, dark, or system theme.
- **About** — app version and links.
