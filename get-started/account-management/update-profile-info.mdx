You can update your personal details including your name and company information from your profile settings. These details help personalize your experience across Relevance AI.

## How to update your profile information

![Personal Details page in Profile settings](/images/profile-personal-details.png)

Follow these steps to update your profile information:

1. **Log into Relevance AI** at https://app.relevanceai.com
2. **Click on your profile picture** in the top right corner, then select **Profile**
3. You will be on the **Personal Details** page
4. **Click the Edit button** on the Personal Information section
5. **Update your first name, last name, and/or company** as needed
6. **Click 'Save Changes'** to update your profile

### About your name

Your name appears throughout Relevance AI in several places:

- When you call an Agent
- What your collaborators see when you interact with shared resources
- What admins see on their Members page
- Throughout the platform when your account is referenced

### About your company

The company field updates only your personal profile and does not affect your organization or project names in Relevance AI.

<Tip>
  To change your organization or project name, please see [Change project and organization name](/get-started/project-management/change-project-and-org-name).
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will changing my name affect my existing projects or agents?">
    No, changing your name will only update how your name is displayed across Relevance AI. All your projects, agents, and resources will remain intact and unchanged.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I change my name as many times as I want?">
    Yes, you can update your name at any time. There are no restrictions on how frequently you can change your display name.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between changing my company here and changing my organization name?">
    The company field on your profile is personal information that doesn't affect your organization or project structure. To change your organization or project name, see [Change project and organization name](/get-started/project-management/change-project-and-org-name).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Will my collaborators be notified when I change my name?">
    No, collaborators will not receive a notification. They will simply see your updated name the next time they interact with you or view shared resources.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I'm having trouble updating my profile information. What should I do?">
    If you're experiencing issues updating your profile, please reach out to our support team [here](https://relevanceai.com/docs/get-started/support).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
