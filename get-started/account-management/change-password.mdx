1. Log out of your account, then head over to our [log in page](https://app.relevanceai.com/auth/reset)
2. Enter your email address
3. Then, click 'Forgot password'
4. Enter your email address again on this page, then click 'Forgot password'
5. You should then be sent an email to change your account password

<Note>
  Adding the ability to change your account's password from within the product is on our roadmap!
