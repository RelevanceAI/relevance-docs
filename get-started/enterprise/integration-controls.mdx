Integration controls allows organization admins and owners to enable integrations that your team can use at the organization level. 

This feature will hide integrations and LLMs that aren't enabled in-app for most users, while organization and project admins an still see all integration options in their respective control pages. 

## How do I set organization-level integration controls?

To set organization-level integration controls, you first must be an organization admin/owner of your Enterprise organization. 

1. Click 'Settings' in the sidebar of Relevance AI
2. Then, click 'Integration controls'
3. From this page, you can quickly toggle all integrations on or off
4. To toggle a specific integration on or off, click the toggle next to the integration
5. You can also use the search bar to find an integration
