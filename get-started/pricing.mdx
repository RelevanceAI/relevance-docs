[Contact sales →](https://relevanceai.com/book-a-demo)

<Note>
  An **Action** is counted when an agent runs a tool — whether it's a simple task like sending one email or a complex workflow with many steps.

  **Vendor Credits** cover LLM and tool usage costs at wholesale (no markup). They roll over indefinitely while you're subscribed, and you can bring your own API keys on paid plans to bypass them entirely.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a Vendor Credit?">
    Credits are used to pay for LLM usage or for third-party API usage by your agents. Your plan includes Vendor Credits that cover most common use cases. Credits roll over and never expire while you're subscribed to a paid plan. You can also bring your own API keys for these vendors if you'd prefer to pay them directly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the BDR agent included?">
    Our curated multi-agent systems like the BDR agent are only available via our sales team. [Book a call](https://relevanceai.com/book-a-demo) to learn more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm interested in setting up an Enterprise plan for my business. Where can I learn more?">
    Learn more about our Enterprise plans with our team. [Book a call](https://relevanceai.com/book-a-demo) to discuss your requirements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to enter a credit card to try Relevance AI?">
    No, Relevance AI's free plan doesn't require a credit card.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does Relevance AI protect my privacy?">
    Your Project Information is confidential, even from Relevance AI. We are SOC 2 Type II compliant. [Read more about Relevance AI Security](/admin/security).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
