The 'Slack - Retrieve Messages' Tool step allows you to retrieve messages from a Slack channel in Relevance AI. 

<Note>You can learn more about the Slack integration [here](/integrations/popular-integrations/slack).</Note>

<Warning>This integration for Slack was developed by our team and is not affiliated with Slack. Please contact our Support for any questions/queries you may have.</Warning>

You can add the 'Slack - Retrieve Messages' Tool step to your Tool by:

1. Creating a new Tool, then searching for the 'Slack: Retrieve Messages' Tool step
2. Click 'Expand' to see the full Tool step
3. Select your Slack account
4. Enter the name of the Slack channel you want to retrieve messages from
5. Specify the number of messages you want to retrieve
6. Test your Tool step by clicking 'Run step'
