### 1. Setting up in the Agent:

1. Create a new agent or edit an existing one.
2. Navigate to the "Triggers" section in "Build" tab.
3. Select "WhatsApp" from the available triggers.
4. Choose the WhatsApp account you want to connect to this agent.
5. Configure trigger condtiions as needed.
6. Save your trigger configuration.

Once set up, your agent will automatically activate when a message is received.

### 2. Setting up in the integrations page

1. Go to the "Integrations" page in the sidebar.
3. In the Integration search input, type "WhatsApp".
4. Click on the "Connect new account" button.
5. In the pop-up window, follow the steps to connect your WhatsApp account.
6. Once connected, you'll see your WhatsApp phone number account listed under Connected Accounts and Triggers.
