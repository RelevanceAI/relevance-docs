### Run a step

Click on the run/play button on the top right of the step to execute it.
Results will be displayed under the tool.

<p align="center">
<img width="500" height="300" align="center" src="/images/build-custom-tools/tool-step/run-a-step.png" />
</p>

Note that there are two options available:
1. Only run the step
2. Rerun from the top including the current step
