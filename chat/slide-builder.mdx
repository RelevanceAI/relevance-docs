## Key Features
- **AI-Generated Content**: Create slide content based on your prompts
- **Dynamic Updates**: Ask Chat to modify or update slides in real-time 
- **Personalized templates**: Upload existing slide templates and Chat will generate slides based off your template
- **Agent Integration**: Work with Relevance AI agents to research or generate content

## Getting Started
1. **Navigate to Slides**: In [Chat](https://chat.relevanceai.com/chat/start), select the Slides feature
2. **Plan your content and slide design**: Look at [Generate content and design the slides](/chat/slide-builder#generating-content-and-designing-slides) for tips
3. **Review and Refine**: Review the generated slides and prompt chat to make changes
4. **Present or Export**: Use the slides directly in Chat or export them for external use

### Generating content and designing slides
1. **Generate Content by using Web Search**: Tell Chat what content you want on your slide deck. Alternatively, use the '@' to ask in your agent for help!
2. **Generate Content by uploading a PDF**: Upload a PDF with specific information you want displayed on the slides
3. **Design your slides using Brand Kit**: Add 3 screenshots of the branding or design you want displayed on the slides 
4. **Use Prompts to Design your Slides**:  Describe what design you want on your slides 

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="How do I create effective slides with AI?">
        Provide clear, specific prompts about your presentation goals, target audience, and key messages. The more context you give, the better the AI can tailor the content and structure.
    </Accordion>
    
    <Accordion title="Can I use my own data in the slides?">
        Yes! You can reference knowledge bases, upload documents, or use agent-generated insights to populate your slides with relevant, up-to-date information.
    </Accordion>
    
    <Accordion title="How many slides can I create in one presentation?">
        There's no strict limit, but we recommend keeping presentations focused and concise. Most effective presentations range from 5-20 slides depending on the topic and audience.
    </Accordion>
    
    <Accordion title="Can I collaborate on slides with my team?">
        Yes, team members in your project can view, comment on, and suggest edits to slides created in shared chat conversations.
    </Accordion>
    
    <Accordion title="What export formats are supported?">
        Chat Slides supports export to PDF, PowerPoint (PPTX), Google Slides, and web-ready HTML formats for maximum compatibility and sharing options.
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
