Relevance AI has multiple channels available for support depending on the tier a customer is on.

- Discord (https://discord.gg/8VHJJrQZMM) available for all users
- Live chat (in-app) available for Pro and above customers
- Slack available for Business and above customers

Our team is available during AEST business hours. We aim to respond to all queries within 24 hours, prioritised by tier.
