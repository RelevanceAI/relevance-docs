## Overview

Reps shouldn't be scrambling for context five minutes before a call. A meeting brief Agent pulls together everything relevant about an account — recent conversations, deal history, company updates — into a snapshot a rep can scan in under a minute.

## What the Agent does

A meeting brief Agent compiles a snapshot of the account ahead of a scheduled call.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Deal context" icon="handshake">
    Stage, value, and key stakeholders on the account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recent activity" icon="clock-rotate-left">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Company updates" icon="newspaper">

  <Card title="Customer signals" icon="ticket">

## How to set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build an Agent">
    [Build an Agent](/build/introduction) with a [prompt](/build/agents/build-your-agent/prompt) that defines your preferred brief format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your CRM">
    Wire up [HubSpot](/integrations/popular-integrations/hubspot) or [Salesforce](/integrations/popular-integrations/salesforce) so the Agent can pull deal and activity data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add email">
    [Gmail](/integrations/popular-integrations/gmail) or [Outlook](/integrations/popular-integrations/outlook) lets the Agent surface recent email threads.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add research tools">
    Web search covers company news and updates outside your own systems.
  </Step>
</Steps>
