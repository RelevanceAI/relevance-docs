## Overview

Generic outreach gets ignored. Personalized messages get replies — but writing them one by one doesn't scale. An outbound Agent uses real prospect context to draft tailored emails and LinkedIn messages so your team can send personalized outreach without the manual effort.

## What the Agent does

An outbound Agent takes prospect data and produces ready-to-send messages.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Cold emails" icon="envelope">
    Personalized opens that reference the prospect's company, role, and recent activity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="LinkedIn outreach" icon="linkedin">
    Connection requests and DMs with relevant context.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Adaptive follow-ups" icon="rotate">
    Sequences that change shape based on what happened on the previous touch.
  </Card>
  <Card title="On-brand voice" icon="comment-dots">
    Messaging that matches your team's tone and value props.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start from a template">
    Clone [LinkedIn Outreach](https://marketplace.relevanceai.com/listing/5508c7f6-f3a7-4593-8faf-22fbecc4e0d4) from the Marketplace, or [build your own](/build/introduction).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your channels">
    Wire up [Gmail](/integrations/popular-integrations/gmail) or [Outlook](/integrations/popular-integrations/outlook) for email, and [LinkedIn](/integrations/popular-integrations/linkedin) for social.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your playbooks">
    Drop messaging playbooks, email templates, and tone-of-voice guides into [Knowledge](/build/knowledge/create-knowledge).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write the prompt">
    A [prompt](/build/agents/build-your-agent/prompt) defines your outreach style, structure, and what personalization to include in every send.
  </Step>
</Steps>
