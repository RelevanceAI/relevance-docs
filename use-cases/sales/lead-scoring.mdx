## Overview

Not every lead deserves the same attention. A scoring Agent evaluates incoming leads against your ICP and buying signals so reps spend their time on the accounts most likely to close.

## What the Agent does

A scoring Agent takes lead data and returns a qualification assessment.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Fit score" icon="bullseye">
    Match against ICP criteria — company size, industry, role, geography.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Intent signals" icon="signal">
    Website activity, content engagement, social behavior, and other buying signals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reasoning" icon="comment-dots">
    A short summary of why the lead scored high or low — not just a number.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recommended action" icon="arrow-right">
    Reach out now, nurture, or disqualify.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build an Agent">
    [Build an Agent](/build/introduction) and define your scoring criteria in the [prompt](/build/agents/build-your-agent/prompt).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your CRM">
    Wire up [HubSpot](/integrations/popular-integrations/hubspot) or [Salesforce](/integrations/popular-integrations/salesforce) so the Agent can pull lead data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your scoring framework">
    Drop your ICP definition and qualification framework into [Knowledge](/build/knowledge/create-knowledge) so every score reflects your team's lens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add research tools">
    Web search lets the Agent look beyond what's already in the CRM when the record is thin.
  </Step>
</Steps>
