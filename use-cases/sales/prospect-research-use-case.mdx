## Overview

Prospect research is one of the most time-consuming parts of any sales workflow. Reps spend hours digging through LinkedIn, company websites, and news articles just to prepare for a single call. An AI agent can do this in seconds.

## What the agent does

A prospect research agent takes a company name or contact and returns a structured brief your rep can scan before a call. This typically includes:

- Company overview, size, industry, and recent funding
- Key contacts and their roles
- Recent news, press releases, or product launches
- Relevant social media activity
- Potential pain points based on industry and company signals
