## Overview

Deals get lost when reps can't speak to how you compare. A competitive intelligence Agent monitors what competitors are doing and keeps your team up to date — so they can position effectively without spending hours on manual research.

## What the Agent does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Product moves" icon="rocket">
    New product launches, feature updates, and pricing changes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Company signals" icon="newspaper">
    Press coverage, funding rounds, and leadership changes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hiring intent" icon="briefcase">
    Job postings that signal strategic direction.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sentiment & social" icon="comments">
    Social media activity and customer sentiment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Battle cards" icon="chess">
    Updated positioning your reps can use in live conversations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build an Agent">
    [Build an Agent](/build/introduction) with a [prompt](/build/agents/build-your-agent/prompt) that defines which competitors to track and what to look for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add monitoring tools">
    Add web search and [Firecrawl](/build/tools/tool-steps/firecrawl) for monitoring competitor websites and news.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload existing knowledge">
    Drop your existing battle cards and positioning docs into [Knowledge](/build/knowledge/create-knowledge) so the Agent builds on what you already have.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wire up delivery">
    Connect [Slack](/integrations/popular-integrations/slack) or [Gmail](/integrations/popular-integrations/gmail) so updates land where the team already works.
  </Step>
</Steps>
