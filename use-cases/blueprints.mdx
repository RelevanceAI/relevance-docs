## How a Blueprint works

Every Blueprint follows the same shape so once you've seen one, you know what to expect from the others:

- **A persona picker at the top** — chat-only, customize a template, or build with code. Pick the one that matches your team's comfort level. The walkthrough below adapts.
- **Real Marketplace Agents** you can clone in one click rather than building from scratch.
- **Specific motions** (e.g. prospect research, outbound) you can implement standalone if you already know what you want to automate.
- **An automation ladder** — L1 assisted up through L4 self-driving — so you can stop at the level that fits and grow into the next when you're ready.
- **Customer stories** from teams already running this in production.
