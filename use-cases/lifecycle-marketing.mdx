## We Commonly See

Marketing teams using AI agents to:

- **Generate lifecycle email campaigns** — draft nurture sequences, re-engagement flows, and onboarding emails that match your brand voice.
- **Create and repurpose content** — turn a single piece of content into blog posts, social snippets, ad copy, and more.
- **Score and segment leads** — automatically categorize inbound leads by intent and fit so the right message reaches the right audience.
- **Analyze campaign performance** — pull together reports across channels and surface what is working without hours of spreadsheet wrangling.
- **Run SEO research** — identify keyword opportunities, audit existing content, and suggest optimizations.
- **Schedule and manage social content** — keep your social calendar full with AI-drafted posts ready for review.
