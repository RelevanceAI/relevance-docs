## About our platform

Relevance AI is a powerful no-code platform that enables businesses to build specialized AI agents, tools, and multiagent systems without extensive development resources. We've transformed the complex process of AI implementation into an accessible strategy that companies of all sizes can leverage to enhance their operations and customer experiences.

At the center of our ecosystem is our rapidly expanding template marketplace - a strategic venue where companies can showcase their products, services, and integrations. By contributing your templates, your company gains visibility within a dynamic community of businesses and individual users actively seeking to deploy AI agents.

## Submission process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Strategic planning">
    Determine which of your products or services would benefit most from marketplace integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submission">
    Complete the comprehensive form with all required business details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & approval">
    Our team will evaluate your submission for technical quality and business value.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publication">
    Once approved, your integration will be published in the marketplace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Performance tracking">
    Monitor adoption metrics, gather user feedback, and refine your offering.
  </Step>
</Steps>
