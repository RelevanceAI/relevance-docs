---
title: "Delete a workforce"
description: "Remove workforces from Relevance AI"
---

You can delete workforces from the Relevance AI interface or from within Inventor. Deleting a workforce is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Delete via the UI

1. Click **Workforces** in the sidebar
2. Click the three-dot menu next to the workforce you want to delete, then select **Delete**
3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears

## Delete via Inventor

You can ask Inventor to delete a workforce during a conversation.

1. Open Inventor from the sidebar
2. Ask Inventor to delete a workforce, for example: "Delete the [workforce name] workforce"
3. Inventor will prompt you to confirm before proceeding — confirmation is always required for deletions, even when auto-approve is enabled for other actions

## Restore a previous workforce version

Inventor can roll an existing workforce back to an earlier version. This restores a previous *version* of a workforce — it does not recover a deleted workforce.

1. Open Inventor from the sidebar
2. Ask Inventor to restore an earlier version, for example: "Restore the previous version of this workforce"
3. Inventor copies the chosen version into the current draft. The live (published) version is unchanged until you publish the draft.
4. Inventor will prompt you to confirm — confirmation is always required for restore actions, even when auto-approve is enabled
