Relevance AI automatically deletes Knowledge tables on inactive accounts to optimize storage. This page explains what triggers deletion, how you are notified, and how to prevent it.

<Warning>
Deletion of inactive Knowledge tables is permanent and cannot be undone. All data stored in those tables will be lost.
</Warning>

## What counts as inactive

Your account is considered inactive when **both** of the following conditions have been true for the last 182 days:

- No tool or agent has been run
- No login to the Relevance AI platform

Both conditions must apply simultaneously. If either condition is not met — for example, if you have logged in within the last 182 days — your account is considered active and your Knowledge tables are safe.

## Warning email

You will receive an email warning 4 days before your Knowledge tables are scheduled for deletion. This email comes from `support@relevanceai.com` and is a legitimate notification from Relevance AI — not spam or phishing.

## What gets deleted

All data stored in your Knowledge tables will be permanently deleted, including all rows, columns, and any documents or files used to populate those tables.

## How to prevent deletion

Log in to Relevance AI and run any Agent or Tool. Either action resets the inactivity timer, and no further steps are needed. You do not need to re-upload data or reconfigure anything — simply using the platform is enough to keep your account active.

If you receive a warning email, log in and run an Agent or Tool before the 4-day window closes to prevent deletion.

## Received the email in error?

If you believe you received a deletion warning in error — for example, if you have been actively using the platform — reply directly to the warning email to open a support ticket. You can also [contact support](/get-started/support) directly.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I recover deleted Knowledge tables?">
    No. Once Knowledge tables are deleted due to inactivity, the data cannot be recovered. If you receive a warning email, log in and run an Agent or Tool before the 4-day window closes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does this policy apply to all accounts?">
    Yes. The inactive Knowledge table deletion policy applies to all Relevance AI accounts regardless of plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What counts as 'running' a tool or agent?">
    Any execution of a Tool or Agent through the Relevance AI platform resets the inactivity timer. This includes manual runs as well as automated or scheduled runs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is inactivity measured?">
    Inactivity is measured independently from two signals: the last tool or agent run, and the last login to the platform. Both must be absent for 182 consecutive days before your account qualifies for deletion. A single login or run within that window is sufficient to prevent it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I receive more than one warning?">
    You will receive at least one warning email 4 days before deletion. Log in and run any Agent or Tool within that window to prevent deletion.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this related to the Enterprise Data Retention feature?">
    No. The inactive Knowledge table deletion policy is a separate, platform-wide policy that applies to all accounts. The [Data Retention](/enterprise/data-retention) feature is an Enterprise-only configuration for managing the lifecycle of tool runs, agent conversations, and other activity data — it does not control Knowledge tables.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
