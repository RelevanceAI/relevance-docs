## Overview

The Google Contacts integration connects your AI agents to your Google Contacts via the People API, providing read-only access to contact data. Agents can retrieve contact details, search your address book, and access contact group information to support workflows that reference or enrich contact data.

<Note>This integration provides read-only access. Creating, updating, or deleting contacts is not supported.</Note>

## Connect the integration

To connect the Google Contacts integration to Relevance AI:

1. Go to the **Integrations & API Keys** page in the sidebar of your Relevance AI dashboard.
2. Click on "Google Contacts" from the available integrations.
3. Click **Connect**.
4. You'll be redirected to Google's authentication page.
5. Select the Google account you want to connect.
6. Review and approve the requested permissions.
7. Once authenticated, you'll be redirected back to Relevance AI with your Google Contacts now connected.

You can connect multiple Google accounts if needed and manage all connections from the Integrations dashboard.

## Tools & Tool Steps

The Google Contacts integration provides actions for retrieving contact information from your Google account.

### Contact retrieval

- **Get Contact**: Retrieve a specific contact by ID or identifier
- **List Contacts**: Retrieve a list of contacts from your Google account
- **Search Contacts**: Find contacts matching specific criteria such as name, email, or phone number

### Contact information

- **Get Contact Details**: Retrieve comprehensive information about a contact, including phone numbers, addresses, and custom fields
- **Get Contact Groups**: List and retrieve the groups your contacts are organized into

## Use the integration's API tool step (advanced)

For use cases requiring access to People API endpoints not covered by the standard actions:

1. Create a new tool in Relevance AI.
2. Scroll down to Tool-steps.
3. Add the Google Contacts API tool-step.
4. Select your connected Google account from the dropdown.
5. Configure the API request with the endpoint, HTTP method, and any parameters.

This gives you direct access to the Google People API, allowing you to query additional contact fields, work with other contacts directories, or implement custom contact retrieval logic.

## Use cases

- **CRM enrichment**: Look up contact details to populate or update CRM records automatically
- **Contact lookup**: Retrieve contact information during email or messaging workflows
- **Automated outreach**: Pull contact details to personalize agent-driven communications
