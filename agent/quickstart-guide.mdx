## Welcome to building your first an Agent

Welcome to the fastest way to get started with Agents\!
Whether you’re new to our platform or looking to launch your next project quickly, this guide will walk you through creating your first Agent in just a few minutes.

## 3 ways to start building your Agent:

Whichever option you choose, you’ll still customize your Agent’s prompt instructions, Tools, and settings to bring it fully to life. Don't worry, we’ll guide you through exactly how in the next article: **Build Your Agent From Scratch.**

    Use our Agent Invent feature to instantly create a functional agent.
    Just describe what you want, and we'll generate a working draft with a prompt and tool placeholders ready for you to customize.
    Get started fast\!
