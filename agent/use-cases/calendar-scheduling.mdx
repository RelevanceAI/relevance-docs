They process thousands of scheduling permutations in seconds while coordinating across teams, time zones, and calendars. No more back-and-forth emails.

To enable your scheduling agent, you'll need two essential integrations:

- **Calendar provider** (Google Calendar or Outlook) with read/write access to check availability and create events
- **Email trigger** (Gmail or Outlook) to monitor your inbox and kick off the agent when scheduling requests arrive

Configure these triggers so your agent can automatically detect scheduling requests and coordinate meetings across your team.

#### How to add these tools to your agent

1. Go to **Tools** in your Agent Builder
2. Click **+ Add Tool**
3. Click **New Tool**
4. Click **Build from Scratch**
5. Click **Add Step**
6. Search for your calendar provider (e.g., "google calendar" or "outlook")
7. Select the tools you need from the results

<Tip>
Search for your specific calendar provider to discover available built-in tools.
