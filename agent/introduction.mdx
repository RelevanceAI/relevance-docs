## What are Agents?

Agents are AI assistants that leverage the power of Large Language Models (LLMs), Tools, and domain-specific Knowledge to tackle complex tasks. 
Agents can be extended and enhanced to tackle virtually any task, allowing you to streamline your workflow and achieve unprecedented levels of efficiency.
Agents are designed to be highly adaptable. They can be equipped with a wide range of [Tools](/get-started/key-concepts/tools) that enable them to handle diverse goals and solve unique challenges.
